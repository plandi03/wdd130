Rocky Rivers - Grand Canyon, USA

Experience the rugged beauty of the Rocky Rivers, winding through majestic canyons and thrilling rapids. Perfect for both adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts.

Bed Rivers - Peaceful Valley, Canada

Relax and rejuvenate with a scenic float down Bed Rivers, known for its serene environment and beautiful forest views. Perfect for beginners and families.

  • Duration: 2 days
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Price: $299 per person

Rosy Rivers - Blossom Valley, Japan

Paddle through the Rosy Rivers, famous for cherry blossom views in spring and vibrant maple colors in the fall. This trip offers both calm stretches and exciting rapids.

  • Duration: 1 day
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Price: $199 per person

Blues Rivers - Smoky Mountains, USA

Explore the deep blue waters of Blues Rivers, with challenging rapids and scenic mountain views. A great choice for those looking for adventure and stunning scenery.

  • Price: $599 per person

Toms Rivers - Wild Gorge, Australia

Get your adrenaline pumping on Toms Rivers with its powerful rapids and rugged landscape. Ideal for experienced rafters looking for a thrilling adventure.

  • Duration: 3 days
  • Difficulty: Expert
  • Price: $799 per person

Thunder Rapids - Iceland

Experience the thrill of Iceland's Thunder Rapids, surrounded by volcanic landscapes and crystal-clear glacial waters. Perfect for adventurers who want a unique and challenging ride.

  • Duration: 5 days
  • Difficulty: Expert
  • Price: $899 per person
